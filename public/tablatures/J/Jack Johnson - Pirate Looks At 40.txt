
A Pirate Looks at 40
Originally by Jimmy Buffett
Covered by Jack Johnson (this version)
Interpreted and Tabbed by Eric-Scott Guthrie


Jack Johnson's version is faithful to the original in terms of chord progression, but instead of a full band playing (and strumming the chords) it's just Jack and his guitar. One of the main differences in the two versions is Jack leaves off the last two verses (finishes with "just a few friends") wheras Jimmy keeps going. Any way you square it though, this is a great song and easy to play.
