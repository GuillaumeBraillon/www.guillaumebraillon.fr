Alright, here is my little tab of one of the best songs off 'Living".  Its a
very emotional song, played with feeling.  I tabbed it according to the live
album version, so the best way to play it is to listen to the song in order 
to
get the feel and the timing.  I've included some time markers, so it will 
help
to play along with a CD player that displays the seconds.

Play it in open C (Low-High  CGCGCE) with a Capo on the 4th Fret.

I'll tab out the nice intro, and include some basic pick patterns, but if 
your
an experienced player, just use a nice picking pattern to hit those open 
notes
every now and then.  As players will see, there is a lot of classic John 
Butler
hammerons (h) and pulloffs (p).  These are done very fast.  Just Listen.
I hope you're a good fingerpicker.
