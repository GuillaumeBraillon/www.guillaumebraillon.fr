Here is the Intro, Verse and Chorus for Cannonball. It's not 100% but again 
just play along with the cd and you should get it, its not as hard as it sounds.

on this song, so I just chose the main guitar part. The only part I didn't tab was 
the break, which comes just after the last chorus. It's just C and D/F# followed 
by the lastbar of the chorus.
