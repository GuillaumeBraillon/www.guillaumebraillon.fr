Eric Clapton - Hey Hey (unplugged)

The trick of this song is to maintain a strickt bass tempo and play the rest of 
the notes around it. I tried to visualise that by keeping the same distance 
between the bass notes and placing the other notes on top of it and in between.

Notice the 'streched' slide '2///7' and '0' below the '7'. The idea is to slide 
in such a tempo that you arive at the 7-th postion just in time to play the next 
bass note (0 on the low E)on top of it.
